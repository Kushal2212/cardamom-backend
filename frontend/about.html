About अलैँची

Inspired by a real problem — using AI to help cardamom farmers in Nepal detect plant diseases early and protect their crops.

🌿

Protecting Nepal's cardamom harvests with the power of AI

Our Mission

Technology in service of farmers

Cardamom (Elettaria cardamomum) is one of Nepal's most valuable cash crops, particularly in the Sankhuwasabha, Ilam, Taplejung, and Panchthar districts. Disease outbreaks like chhirke can devastate an entire season's harvest.

अलैँची was built to give farmers a fast, free, and accurate tool to identify diseases before they spread — using only a smartphone photo and an internet connection.

Our model is trained on field-collected images from Nepali farms, making it more relevant and accurate for local conditions than generic plant disease tools.

Development Journey

Project Timeline

Phase 1 · Research

Problem Identification & Literature Review

Studied cardamom diseases, surveyed farmers in Sankhuwasabha district, and reviewed existing plant pathology AI research.

Phase 2 · Data Collection

Field Image Dataset

Collected and annotated 1500+ leaf images across 3 disease categories from real Nepali cardamom farms.

Phase 3 · Model Training

EfficientNetB0 & MobileNetV2 Fine-tuning

Transfer-learned EfficientNetB0 and MobileNetV2 on the local dataset, achieving 98.54% accuracy after augmentation and tuning.

Phase 4 · Development

Full-Stack Web Application

Built Flask REST API, SQLite database, JWT authentication, and the bilingual frontend interface.

Phase 5 · Deployment

Live System & Farmer Testing

Deployed and tested with real farmers — collecting feedback to improve accuracy and usability.
