Quick Questions

Is अलैँची free to use? +
Yes, completely free. Create an account and start detecting diseases at no cost.
What image formats are supported? +
JPG, PNG, and BMP formats are supported. For best results, ensure the leaf is well-lit and occupies most of the frame.
Can I use it offline? +
Currently no — it requires an internet connection to send the image to the server for analysis. Offline mobile support is planned.
How accurate is the model? +
Our EfficientNetB0 and MobileNetV2 models achieve 97% accuracy on the test dataset collected from Nepali farms.
